Program

A program built with the community

In keeping with DISI's collective spirit, the full week takes shape together with participants once accepted. Below is a general proposal for the opening day — the rest of the program will be co-designed and announced as it develops.

Day 1

Arrival & Welcome

Morning

Registration & welcome gathering

Arrival, registration, and an informal welcome breakfast featuring local Oaxacan food, giving everyone a first chance to meet.

Mid-morning

Opening ceremony

Welcome remarks from the organizing team and from representatives of local Oaxacan communities, framing the place and context we are gathering in.

Late morning

Keynote: What is DISI Global?

An introductory talk by the organizers on the DISI network, and on the guiding question of this Oaxaca edition: how does an intelligent relationship between communities and their environment emerge and evolve within specific ecological and cultural contexts?

Afternoon

Community-building activity

A guided icebreaker grounded in the DISI pillars — generating bonds, identities, and shared meaning, and breaking down the barrier of the unfamiliar.

Late afternoon

Orientation to the collective projects

Introduction to the single collective research project and the single collective storytelling project, and to how each participant's proposed contribution fits in.

Evening

Welcome dinner

A shared meal celebrating Oaxacan cuisine, music, and hospitality — the first of many informal spaces for dialogue.
