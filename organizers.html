About DISI

The Diverse Intelligences Summer Institute

DISI is a community-building initiative exploring "the origins, nature, and future of intelligences" — diverse manifestations of mind and cognition, of agency and awareness, across species and systems. It brings together early-career scholars and storytellers, deliberately breaking down traditional disciplinary boundaries through transdisciplinary lectures, seminars, projects, meals, and salons.

DISI is held annually in St Andrews, Scotland, and has grown a global network of DISI Global events that carry its community-first ethos into new ecological and cultural contexts around the world — of which this Oaxaca edition is one.
