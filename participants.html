Beyond the week

The DISI alumni network

Being part of DISI doesn't end when the event does. DISI fosters a lifelong, worldwide network of alumni — fellows and faculty who continue to collaborate, share opportunities, and stay in dialogue well beyond their time together, through year-round programming and community connections.

DISI Global Oaxaca fellows will join this broader DISI alumni community, alongside participants from DISI events around the world.

Local participation, on your own terms. Members of Oaxacan communities are warmly invited to take part in DISI Global Oaxaca, with no obligation to remain involved beyond what feels meaningful to them — participation is always a choice, not an expectation.
