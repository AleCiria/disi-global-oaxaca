A region of rich diversity

Why Oaxaca

Oaxaca's biological and cultural diversity — and the relationships its communities have developed with their environment over generations — is central to this DISI Global event. Participants can expect an experience shaped by local ecological knowledge, community organization, linguistic diversity, and agroecological practice, alongside the more conventional elements of an academic gathering.

Stay tuned: logistics — venue, accommodation partners, transportation, and accessibility information — will be updated on this page as the event is organized. Accepted participants will also receive details directly by email.
